Legal & Support

Worker Rights

All workers have the right to fair treatment, safe working conditions, and timely payment.

For more information, please refer to the following resources:

  • International Labour Organization (ILO)
  • International Organization for Migration (IOM)
  • Local Laws and Regulations

Dispute Resolution

If you have any disputes regarding your employment, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact your employer to discuss the issue.
  2. If unresolved, seek assistance from a legal aid organization.
  3. Consider mediation or arbitration as a resolution method.
